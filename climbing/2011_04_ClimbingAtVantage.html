2011_04_ClimbingAtVantage

Written by Steven Monday, 02 May 2011 19:34

This last weekend, Tara and I went climbing/camping at Vantage (aka Frenchmen Coulee).  We drove up Friday afternoon after work and got there around 7:30 only to find out we needed a parking permit.  Well, all of the close stores were closed so we drove into Ephrata and got a permit at Wal-Mart.  We then proceeded back to Vantage and found a nice spot next to the road in the bivuac section to camp.  We woke up the next morning and had some taters, eggs and toast to fuel a day of climbing!  We started at the feathers (right near the camp area) and climbed a couple of routes there before lunch.  We then hiked over to the Sunshine Wall and hike almost the whole way down it until we found the right conditions ( #1 - Not to many people and #2 - Routes there we could climb).  We ended up climbing three routes at the end and had a blast! Check back soon for a new video (no promises when it will be done though)!
