2011_03_Vantage

Written by Steven Sunday, 13 March 2011 20:40

Last weekend Sarah (a local friend), Tara and I went for our first climbing trip of the year.  We went to Vantage (also known as Frenchmen Coulee).  It was a first for me, but Tara and Sarah had been there before.  The geology of the area is incredible and the basalt columns are really impressive.  We are definetely going to go back!
