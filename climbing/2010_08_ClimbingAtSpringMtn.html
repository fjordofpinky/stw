2010_08_ClimbingAtSpringMtn

Written by Steven Thursday, 23 September 2010 19:11

About a month ago, Tara, Billy and I went on a camping/climbing/wood collecting trip.  However, our trip ending up having some problems.  Tara and I were going to do a practice hike for our big hike the following weekend with my co-worker.  But when we stopped about 4 miles from where we were going to camp, I heard the sound of air whooshing out of the back left tire!  I got down and sure enough there was a hole in the tire.  We quickly turned around and headed back towards civilization.  We got back to the little town of Meacham, OR.  Where we called AAA and they came to put our spare on because I didn't have a tire iron to remove the lugs =(  So, we went back home and spent the night and got up really early (to take the garbage to the dump...Alice's Resturant anybody???) and got tires at Les Schwab and went back up to the mountains to finish our trip.  So we got up there and spent a few hours climbing on the rocks and then came back and cut down a few trees for firewood.  Then we had a nice relaxing meal of spaghetti and baked beans...the perfect combo!  Tara and I got up the next morning (Billy slept in because he didn't have a good nights rest) and went to climb a few more routes on the rock.  All in all it was a fun weekend...even though we did two trips instead of one!
