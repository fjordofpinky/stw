2011_08_FloatinTheWallaWalla

Written by Steven Saturday, 27 August 2011 18:58

Today, Tara and I went out with Billy and Jen to float the Walla Walla River.  We originally thought about floating the Wallowa River, but decided we didn't want to drive that far.  It's been HOT here the last week or so and the cold river felt nice (although Tara actually got cold).  You'll notice in the video that Tara kinda got jipped with the small tube...part of the reason why she was cold is that she was in the water more than anyone else!  We found that the reach between Couse Creek Bridge (where we take the dogs to play) and Grove School Bridge (just 3 blocks from our house) is a really fun section.  We can float that part of the river after work to cool down!
