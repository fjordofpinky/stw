2011_05_PatioBuilding

Written by Steven Sunday, 22 May 2011 18:29

Well, after planning it for a long time and putting off working on it for a long time, we finally got around to building our new patio in the back yard.  We tore out the old, rotten deck last month and hauled it away to the dump on Free-Dump day!  Then we let that area become the dogs new favorite digging area for about a month.  After buying some used brick and figuring out how we would "box-in" the bricks, we finally "worked" on it today.  We are not totally done, we will be building a small outdoor counter/sink along the house so we can use it when we BBQ.  Also, we are going to build a pergola over the patio to shade us (mostly Steven) from the hot Walla Walla (aka Milton-Freewater) sun!
