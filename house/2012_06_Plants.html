2012_06_Plants

Written by Steven Saturday, 23 June 2012 19:29

Summer is coming right along and with it our plants are growing.  We've planted the garden and hopefully soon will be harvesting from it.  We also planted a number of new things this year in our yard.  A contorted larch is now gracing our back yard and a juniper tree is in the front yard to hopefully survive the hot, dry area that has killed two trees already!  We also have raspberries & strawberries, but no blackberries (they are invasive - we've pulled them all).  We also barked the flower beds around the house - what difference a little (or a lot) bark will make.
