2010_08_ChimneyLake

Written by Steven Saturday, 04 September 2010 22:52

A couple weekends ago, Tara and I went on a little hike with one of my co-workers (Troy) in the Eagle Cap Wilderness near Joseph, OR.  We got to the trailhead around 3:30 and headed out on the trail around 4:00.  We reached our campsite around 7:00 after hiking around 5 miles and gaining 3500 feet in elevation!  It was a beautiful place, we camped right next to a crystal clear lake with granite talus field surrounding it.  The next day Tara and Troy went on a little hike to summit three of the mountains near there.  Troy is in the process of climbing Oregon's 100 highest peaks...these were three that were on the list.  While they did that hiking, I was busy taking photos...something like ~760 of them!  We had a great time and the dogs REALLY had a great time.  They climbed the mountains with Troy and Tara and when they got back to camp were ready to play fetch and swim in the lake.  We hiked out and got some much needed deep-fried fish at a local hole in the wall on the way home.  Tara and I also stopped to get some firewood on our way home...we didn't end up getting a very big load because we were both pretty wasted.
